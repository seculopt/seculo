The Problem

Finding a property shouldn't feel like a second job

Finding a home means checking ImóVirtual, Idealista, SuperCasa, and dozens of other portals — one by one. Listings overlap, filters differ, and you can easily miss the perfect option buried on a smaller site.

  • ⏱ Hours lost switching between portals
  • 📋 The same listing on five different sites, at five different prices
  • 🔍 Great options hidden on smaller, lesser-known portals
  • 📊 No way to see and compare everything side by side and actually decide
