The Problem

The Portuguese property market is fragmented.

Finding the right property means jumping between ImóVirtual, Idealista, SuperCasa, and dozens of regional portals separately. Losing time, dealing with duplicate listings, and comparing data manually.

  • ⏱ Hours lost switching between portals
  • 📋 Inconsistent filters across platforms
  • 📊 No way to export and compare at scale

How Século Explorer Works

01

Set your parameters

Define zone, property type, price, area, bedrooms, and required features. Every filter maps directly to the underlying portal APIs.

02

Search 11 portals at once

One click aggregates ImóVirtual, SuperCasa, and 9 secondary portals. Results are deduplicated and ranked by match score.

03

Export and decide

Download a structured Excel or CSV file with URL, price, area, photos, and contact info. Integrated map view included.
